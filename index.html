Ziyue Xu (徐子岳)

I am an undergraduate student in the 8-year Clinical Medicine program at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Yi-zhou Jiang.

Over the past three years, I have been conducting research on "Precision Subtyping and Targeted Therapy of Breast Cancer". I have published 7 SCI papers in journals such as BMJ Oncology, Journal of Pathology, and British Journal of Cancer, with 1 paper as (co-)first author, accumulating a total impact factor of 34.8. My research has been selected for oral presentation and poster display at the 2024 European Association of Urology (EAU) Annual Congress.

I have extensive research experience, professional skills, and strong enthusiasm in molecular subtyping, targeted therapy, and precision prevention of solid tumors. I look forward to continuing in-depth exploration in future research work.

Identified clinical and molecular predictors of response to disitamab vedotin, a HER-2-targeting antibody-drug conjugate, in metastatic breast cancer patients. Patients with higher HER-2 expression or elevated tumor mutational burden showed better clinical outcomes.

Gender disparities in clinical outcomes of breast cancer linked to X chromosome gene KDM6A mutation
Zhaopei Liu*, Kaifeng Jin*, Ziyue Xu*, Jingtong Xu*, Xiaohe Su, Bingyu Li, Ge Liu, Hailong Liu, Yuan Chang, Yiwei Wang, Le Xu, Weijuan Zhang, Zewei Wang†, Yu Zhu†, Jiejie Xu†
BMJ Oncology, 2023;2:e000199 EAU Oral Presentation

Discovered that histone demethylase KDM6A mutation plays a crucial role in mediating gender disparities in breast cancer prognosis. The mutation can further activate estrogen receptors and reduce immune infiltration, leading to extremely poor outcomes in female patients while having opposite effects in male patients.
